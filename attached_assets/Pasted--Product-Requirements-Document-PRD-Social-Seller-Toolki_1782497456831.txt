# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Social Seller Toolkit – Content Library & Customer Portal

## Product Overview

The Content Library & Customer Portal is an extension of the Social Seller Toolkit designed to solve two major problems faced by social media businesses:

1. Sellers struggle to consistently create engaging captions and broadcast messages.
2. Customers have no centralized place to track their orders after purchasing through WhatsApp or Instagram.

The module combines an AI-assisted content library with a customer portal to improve both sales and customer experience.

---

# Problem Statement

Most social sellers spend hours:

* Thinking of captions every day.
* Writing promotional broadcasts.
* Creating sales campaigns.
* Responding to customers asking:

  * "Has my order been shipped?"
  * "Where is my package?"
  * "Can I see my previous orders?"

There is no centralized solution that handles both marketing content and customer order tracking.

---

# Product Goals

The product should enable sellers to:

* Generate sales content in seconds.
* Access ready-made templates.
* Increase engagement.
* Improve repeat purchases.
* Reduce customer support workload.
* Give customers confidence through transparent order tracking.
